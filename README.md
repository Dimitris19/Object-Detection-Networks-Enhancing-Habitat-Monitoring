# Object-Detection-Networks-Enhancing-Habitat-Monitoring

This page presents visualizations of experiments using the Faster R-CNN and YOLO models from the paper titled "Unlocking River Secrets: Environmental Flows Meet Object Detection Networks for Enhanced Habitat Monitoring. A YOLOv8 model and a Faster-RCNN are trained for 100 epochs with a batch size of 16. We used K = 5 different partitions of the dataset, conducting an equal number of experiments for each. In our experimental setup, the dataset was divided into three subsets of training, validation and test data with the proportions of 60%, 20% and 20% respectively. For YOLO, an Adam optimizer was used with a learning rate of 10−3 while for Faster RCNN, the AdamW optimizer with a learning rate of 10−3 yielded the best results.
